TrustGuard is built to keep your account safe from the stuff most people don’t even see coming. It actively blocks session stealers and prevents tools that try to grab your UUID or username from working in the first place. Instead of reacting after damage is done, it shuts down those attempts before they go anywhere.

The mod runs quietly in the background and doesn’t get in your way, but it’s always watching for anything suspicious. If something tries to access or leak your session data, it gets stopped instantly.

Simple, lightweight, and focused on one thing: making sure your account stays yours.
